FAQ

What is SignalGuard?

A source-grounded claim review contract that fetches a public source and stores a constrained verdict.

How can builders inspect it?

Start with the project overview, then use the tutorial and reviewer quickstart.

Does this guarantee points?

No. Portal review decides awards. The public materials are designed to be reviewable, not to bypass review.

What should a builder reuse?

The source URL discipline, small verdict vocabulary, evidence hub pattern, and verifier workflow.
