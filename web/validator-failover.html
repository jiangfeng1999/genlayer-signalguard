Drill Steps

Trigger Check Action
Health endpoint reports down. GET /health on active and standby nodes. Confirm standby health before switching.
Metrics scrape is missing or stale. GET /metrics and compare scrape age. Switch monitoring target and verify collectors.
Operator balance is below threshold. GET /balance and confirm account threshold. Route writes away from the low-balance operator.
Snapshot needed before maintenance. POST /snapshot with protected operator token. Create a snapshot before failover or upgrade.
